About

Little Angel's

At Little Angel's, we recognize and cultivate each student’s strengths and talents, while providing individualized instruction to meet his or her unique learning needs.
Our unique approach of embedding therapeutic supports throughout the academic school day allows us to provide students with an environment in which they feel safe to learn, grow, and build positive relationships. Each student at Little Angel's receives individualized instruction as well as targeted intervention using specialized methodologies when appropriate. Emphasis is placed on college and career readiness, and the development of executive functioning skills for all students.

Our Vision

The agenda of education at L.A.P.S is to focus more on the formation of the youth rather than just imparting knowledge.
