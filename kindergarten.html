Kindergarten

"All children are unique and gifted - they just unwrap their packages at different time."

A kindergarten means "Children's Garden", a preschool educational approach where the children are nurtured and nourishedâ€™ like plants in a garden. Playing, singing, practical activities, and social interaction make this transition easy from home to school.

The child steps out from the closed environment of his / her home to the unknown world of school. He / she is little nervous to accept the world. The right environment and support can help him / her to become comfortable and certainly can give him / her a better future. We look forward to emphasize on habit formation, socializing and guiding them to learn to communicate in unknown boundaries. A child must be instilled with the skill of expressing himself / herself.

Learning and growing should be a fun. We believe that a budding minds can absorb an amazing amount of information and concepts especially when all this is presented in a positive manner and in a stress free conducive atmosphere. The need is to develop a surrounding which is full of fun and devoid of any threats or embarrassment. We want them to feel protected and grow as independent individuals. Our aim is to expose children to the kind of stimulating experiences with healthy mental growth, motor development and writing readiness.

Fun with numbers develops analytical and logical reasoning. At pre-primary stage the expected outcome can be summarized as: cognitive development, enhancement of vocabulary, inter-personal skills, and readiness, recognition of numbers, promotion of knowledge, understanding, and development of personal & social skills, gross motor development, age appropriate values and habits.

Play Group

Welcome To the very first class of your ward,here your kid will learn so many things all around.

Your kid will be introduced with colors, shapes, vegetables, fruits, numbers, alphabets, good habits, dinning mannerism, behavioural facts, greetings and of course gripping/holding.

Nursery

Nursery classes is actually a base of future academic. Now, kids are more active and ready to understand things correctly.

We have multiple activities to make them learn the school curriculum including art and craft and outdoor activities. We engage them with other kids at school and together they explore toys, share things etc.

Lower Kindergarten

Kids are more disciplined now. So, many things happen here very good oral part, games, general awareness, and written work given to kids.

The syllabus is prepared to stimulate and meet the child curiosity this level concentrated further social skills and prepares kid to come up in-crowd and prove themselves in collecting good and social habits.

Upper Kindergarten

UKG kids are prepared to move for full-day formal school, now they are geniuses and can do their work by themselves.

So many social activities are done in this class, teachers encourage readiness to learn and question, utilization of the children's developing sense of responsibility and their interest in play.
