About Little Angel's

"Educating all students to achieve today and tomorrow in a global community and economy."

Lt. Sr. Harmel Singh

Founder

Little Angel's Public School

The planning of an English Medium School was made by Lt. Sr. Harmel Singh because of the growing demand of an English Medium School this locality. It resulted with the inception of the school under the banner of ‘Little Angel’s Public School’ which officially started on 5th April 2003 with a batch of mere 50 children for Nursery, KG and standard one. Thus, the long-coveted dream to provide value-based education to the poorer as well as privileged people with the noble aim has geared up.

This institute has seen many ups and downs to reach this status that it enjoys today. In the long history of this institute, it has experienced many adverse circumstances but thanks to Lt. Sr. Harmel Singh who has blessed it with fortitude and reticent to thwart those crises and proceed to its vernal journey.

Why Choose Little Angel's?

LAPS is one of the best schools where we provide ample scope for physical, intellectual, and social development of students. Our school provide students with access to innovative programs that engage and tap into their specific interests, aspirations, and preferred learning styles.

Quality Teachers

Having excellent, competent and knowledgeable teachers is important. At LAPS, we make sure that our students have access to the best teaching they can possibly have by only using highly-qualified, experienced teachers.

Project-based and service-learning

Students at all grade levels may participate in instruction built upon authentic learning experiences that engage student interest and motivation with meaningful service to the community.
