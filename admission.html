Admission Procedure

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

Little Angel’s Public School welcomes applications for admission from all students regardless of nationality, religion, cast,e or gender. To seek Admission to Little Angel’s Public School, Pilibanga, one needs to register On-Line Registration. After the submission of the registration form, take a print out and the same has to be submitted to the school City Office. For Offline Registration, take the Registration Form from School Reception, fill all the entries and submit the form.

Student Prospectus & form Fee - 1000 /-

PLEASE NOTE: Any cause for failure in reporting school office within 48 hours will not be entertained. The registration does not guarantee admission.

AFTER REGISTRATION:

1. After registration, you will get a copy of Prospectus along with the Application form from the administration office.

2. The application form is to be filled in & submitted to the school's administrative office within two working days of the issue of the form.

The following documents have to be submitted to the Administrative office along with a dully-filled admission form:

  • Recent Four (4) Passport size colored photographs.
  • One recent Passport size photographs each of both the parents.
  • Birth certificate issued by Municipal Corporation or other authorized competent authority.
  • Photocopy of last school's Exam report card / Midterm report card.
    (Not applicable for LKG)

Incomplete forms without the necessary documents / information will be rejected.

3. Admission to class LKG & UKG will be through Observation / Interaction. Dates of interaction will be intimated.

4. Admission to class 1st onwards will be through a written Admission test. The selection of the candidates will depend upon the child's performance in the Test / Interview and availability of seats in the concerned class.

5. Assessment of the child for admission is also based on the following factors:

  • Academic readiness and motivation.
  • Social and emotional development.
  • Ability of the school to serve the needs of the child.

6. The result date of the Admission test & the last date for the submission of fee will be specified at the time of the test / interview.

The School reserves the right to admit any student who is found fit for admission or refuse admission without assigning any reason. For all matters related to admissions, the decision of the Principal will be final.

AT THE TIME OF ADMISSIONS:

1. The list of the selected students as per the admission procedure will be displayed on the notice board & website.

2. Students offered admission must complete the following formalities within one week time Otherwise the offer will stand cancelled and the seat will be passed on to the next student in the 'Waiting List'.

3. The following documents are to be submitted at the time of admission:

  • Birth certificate.
  • Evidence of passing the last class or promotion to present class.
    (Not applicable for LKG)
  • Original Transfer Certificate from the previous school, which should be a recognized school, affiliated to the All India Board or a State Board of Education.
    (If Transfer Certificate is issued by other state, it must be duly signed by DEO.)
4. Passport size photographs.

5. Proof of residence of the child (Copy of Ration card / Passport OR Voter's ID of the parents).

6. In case a child is adopted, parents will have to submit an attested copy of the adoption certificate issued by the competent authority. In case of divorce/separation, the parent who is admitting the child will have to submit a copy of the custody of the child certificate issued by the competent law officer.

PAYMENT OF FEE:

1. Full payment of the fee and dues as payable should be paid by a new student at the time of admission as per the schedule laid down by the school. Payment can be made through a bank draft or cheque. Demand Draft should be made in the name of "Little Angel’s Public School” payable at Pilibanga.

2. The fee payable is subject to change from time to time. Parents will however be informed of any such changes.

3. In case of withdrawal, the Caution Money will be refunded subject to the clearance of all dues within one month time.

Admission will be valid only if the fee is paid by the due date along with the necessary documents and certificates as mentioned above and on completion of all formalities. Admission to Little Angel’s Public School implies on the part of students and parents willingness to comply with the requirements, rules, and regulations of the school.
